A Mutual Friend for Everyone

With Uni+, making friends couldn't be easier. Think of Uni+ as a mutual friend who can introduce you to others as amazing as you are. Just start a one-on-one conversation with a single tap. We'll even suggest an opening line.

Uni+ is Unique

Unlike other social apps, Uni+ isn't meant for connecting with existing friends. Instead, Uni+ helps you discover new people in a fun and easy way. You don't need to search for people and scroll through pictures of cats.
